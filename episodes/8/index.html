About the Show

The Five-Minute Geek show is 5-10 minutes of geek, twice a week.

It's a podcast (and video cast) for developers and anyone else who lives in our world--frontend, backend, project management, audio, design, whatever. If it's geeky, it fits!

Who's Matt?

Matt is @stauffermatt on Twitter, writes and speaks at Mattstauffer.co and is partner & technical director at Tighten Co.
